Henry Lunt Family

Family trees for Henry Lunt, his four wives, and their descendants

Henry Lunt married four wives over the course of his life, in the practice of plural marriage common among early members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Together they raised a large family whose descendants span many generations. Click any name below to view the family tree document in the viewer.

Wife 1 — Ellen Whittaker

Ellen Whittaker

1830 – 1903 Married: 25 March 1852, Parowan, Utah

Henry's first wife. Ellen remained a devoted partner through the early pioneer years in Cedar City, and later in Mexico where Henry's fourth wife, Sarah, cared for her in her final years alongside Henry himself.

Wife 4 — Sarah Ann Lunt

Sarah Ann Lunt

1858 – 1921 Married: 16 January 1878, St. George, Utah

Henry's fourth and youngest wife. Sarah was a remarkable woman who managed the hotel and stage stop while Henry served a mission, cared for Henry and his other wives through old age, and guided the family through the difficult exodus to Mexico. Her cheese-making in Colonia Pacheco became famous in the region.
