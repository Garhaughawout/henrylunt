About This Biography

This biography of Henry Lunt was compiled by the Henry Lunt Family Organization. It spans 30 chapters, tracing his life from birth in Cheshire, England in 1824 through his conversion to the LDS faith, his emigration to America, decades of pioneer service in Cedar City, Utah, and his final years in Colonia Pacheco, Mexico.
