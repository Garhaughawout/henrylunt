Because Dad died suddenly before my twin and I were born, I never knew Dad. But, I am grateful for the good man he was as evidenced by what he wrote to his older brother, Broughton, while living in El Paso after the Saints were driven out of Mexico. Uncle Broughton was serving a mission at the time.
Dad wrote, “I never did appreciate the gospel so much before; there is a comfort it brings which sorrows cannot sting. I only hope that our trials will result in the development of a strong character. We can conquer or be conquered and if we are conquered by conditions, we have failed in the purpose for which we were placed on earth.”
Terry Lunt
