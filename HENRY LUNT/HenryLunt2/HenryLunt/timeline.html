Timeline of Henry Lunt's Life ↓

Scroll down to follow the key events from 1824 to 1902

  • 1824 Birth: 20 July 1824, Cheshire, England — map
  • 1824 Christening & Residence: 1 August 1824, Wrenbury, Cheshire, England — map
  • 1836 Death of Father: 19 September 1836 — Randle Lunt (1766–1836), Badenhall, Staffordshire, England
  • 1849 Baptism into The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints: 6 October 1849
  • 1850 Overland Emigration: Milo Andrus Company, 3 June 1850 — crossed the plains to Utah
  • 1850 Arrived in Utah: 30 August 1850 — map
  • 1852 Marriage: 25 March 1852, Parowan, Iron, Utah — Ellen Whittaker (1830–1903)
  • c. 1853 Birth of Child: Ammon Lunt (1853–1870), Cedar City, Utah
  • c. 1856 Birth of Child: Ann Lunt (1856–1865), Cedar City, Utah
  • 1854–1857 Mission to England: Served as a missionary in his homeland
  • 1857 Returned to Utah: June 1857 and 26 September 1857
  • 1857 Marriage: 7 October 1857, Salt Lake City, Utah — Mary Ann Wilson (1834–1910)
  • 1858 Birth of Child: Martha Henrietta Lunt (1858–1932), Cedar City
  • 1861 Birth of Child: Ellen Eva Wilson Lunt (1861–1923), Cedar City
  • 1862 Death of Mother: 7 March 1862 — Anne Owen (1781–1862), Wybunbury, Cheshire, England
  • 1863 Birth of Child: Henry Whitaker Wilson Lunt (1863–1926), Cedar City
  • 1863 Marriage: 11 April 1863, Salt Lake City, Utah — Ann Cresswell Gower (1843–1914)
  • 1864 Birth of Children: Jane Gower Lunt (1864–1952) & Randle Wilson Lunt (1864–1951), Cedar City
  • 1865 Death of Child: Ann Lunt (1856–1865), Cedar City
  • 1866 Birth of Child: Jemima Gower Lunt (1866–1945), Cedar City
  • 1867 Birth of Child: William Wilson Lunt (1867–1930), Cedar City
  • 1868 Birth of Child: Roselia Gower Lunt (1868–1928), Cedar City
  • 1870 Deaths & Births: Ammon Lunt died (4 Jan 1870); Florence Wilson Lunt born (20 Jan 1870); Oscar Gower Lunt born (6 Oct 1870), Cedar City
  • 1873 Birth of Child: Violet Wilson Lunt (1873–1967), Cedar City
  • 1874 Birth of Child: George Albert Lunt (1874–1960), Cedar City
  • 1875 Birth of Child: Alice Maude Wilson Lunt (1875–1962), Cedar City
  • 1878 Marriage: 16 January 1878, St. George, Utah — Sarah Ann Lunt (1858–1921)
  • 1878 Birth of Child: Thomas Amos Lunt (1878–1947), Cedar City
  • 1879 Birth of Child: Egerton Lunt (1879–1918), Cedar City
  • 1880 Birth of Child: Ellen Gower Lunt (1880–1945), Cedar City
  • 1881 Birth of Child: Broughton Lunt (1881–1946), Cedar City
  • 1883 Indians Mission, 1883; Set Apart: 9 April 1883 — Birth of Children: Rachel Ann Lunt & Parley "L" Lunt (both 1883), Cedar City / Johnson Springs
  • 1885 Birth of Child: Edward Lunt (1885–1959), Nephi, Juab, Utah Territory
  • 1886 Mission to England again, 1886 — Taking one wife to England to avoid polygamy prosecution
  • 1887 Court proceedings, Utah — facing federal charges for polygamy
  • 1887 Left Cedar City: November 1887 — began journey to Mexico with Sarah's family
  • 1888 Birth of Children: Heaton Lunt (7 Mar 1888, Arizona Territory) & Frank George Lunt (16 Mar 1888, Buenaventura, Chihuahua, Mexico)
  • 1889 Arrived at Colonia Diaz, Chihuahua, Mexico: December 1889 — map
  • 1890 Arrived at Colonia Pacheco: January 1890. Engaged in farming and ranching; Sarah's cheese became famous — map
  • 1890 Birth of Child: Alma L Lunt (1890–1957), Colonia Pacheco, Mexico
  • 1892 Birth of Child: Owen Lunt (1892–1934), Colonia Pacheco, Mexico
  • 1893 Sealing to Spouse: 30 May 1893, St. George Utah Temple
  • 1896 Birth of Child: Clarence L Lunt (1896–1939), Colonia Pacheco, Mexico
  • 1902 Death: 22 January 1902, Colonia Pacheco, Casas Grandes, Chihuahua, Mexico — map
  • 1902 Burial: 24 January 1902, Colonia Pacheco Cemetery, Chihuahua, Mexico
